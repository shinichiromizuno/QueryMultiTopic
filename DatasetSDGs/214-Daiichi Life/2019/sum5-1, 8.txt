To leverage the real estate resources of the Dai-ichi Life Group and further expand its Asset Management Business, the Group and Sohgo Housing Co., Ltd. jointly established a new company called Dai-ichi Life Realty Asset Management Co., Ltd. in July 2019.
By the first half of fiscal 2020, this company seeks to form private-placement REITs whose investment targets will be real estate that contributes to the improvement of quality of life at the residences and for the people who inhabit them. Taking advantage of the real estate investment expertise of the Dai-ichi Life Group, Dai-ichi Life Realty Asset Management will offer high-quality investment opportunities to institutional investors in Japan.
